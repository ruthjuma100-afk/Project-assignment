About Luka

Luka is a loving and energetic dog who brings happiness everywhere he goes. He is curious, playful, and always ready for an adventure. From the day he came into my life, he became part of my family. His presence makes every day brighter and more enjoyable.

Luka’s favorite things include long walks, tasty treats, playing fetch, and relaxing in the sun. He especially loves being outdoors and spending time with the people he loves.
